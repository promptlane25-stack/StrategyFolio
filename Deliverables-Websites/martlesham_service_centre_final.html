FAQ

Common Questions

Answers to the things customers ask us most.

How long does an MOT take?
A standard MOT takes approximately 45–60 minutes. We carry them out while you wait, so you're welcome to stay in our waiting area. If you'd prefer, we also have a courtesy car available.
Can you collect my vehicle from home or work?
Yes — just let us know when you make your booking and we'll arrange a collection time that suits you. This service is available locally around the Martlesham Heath and Ipswich area.
What happens if my car fails its MOT?
We offer a free re-test if your vehicle fails. We'll clearly explain what needs to be fixed, give you a written quote, and only proceed with repairs once you've approved the work.
My car is undriveable — what can I do?
Don't worry. We offer a local recovery service and can bring your vehicle to our workshop. Just call us on 01473 631111 and we'll get you sorted as quickly as possible.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit and debit cards, cash, and bank transfer by BACS. Payment is taken on collection of the vehicle once all work is complete.
Do you work on all makes and models?
Yes — our technicians are experienced across all makes and models including Ford, Vauxhall, Volkswagen, Audi, BMW, Toyota, Honda, Nissan and more. If you're unsure, just call us.
How do I know if my car needs a service?
As a rule of thumb, an interim service is recommended every 6 months or 6,000 miles, and a full service annually or every 12,000 miles. Your dashboard service light may also indicate when one is due. Give us a call if you're unsure.
Is a courtesy car always available?
We have a courtesy car available for customers who need it — subject to availability. Please request this when making your booking so we can ensure it's reserved for you.
