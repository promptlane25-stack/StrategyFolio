About Us

A Family Business Built on Trust

Martlesham Service Centre is a family-run garage based in Martlesham Heath, Ipswich. Father, son and daughter run the business together — between them, over 45 years of experience in the motor industry.

We've been serving drivers across Ipswich, Woodbridge, Kesgrave, Rushmere and the surrounding villages since we opened. Our reputation is built on one thing: treating every customer the way we'd want to be treated ourselves.

No jargon, no unnecessary upsells, no surprises on the invoice. Just honest, quality work from people who genuinely care.

FAQ

Common Questions

Answers to the things customers ask us most.

Can you collect my vehicle from home or work?
Yes — just let us know when you make your booking and we'll arrange a collection time that suits you. Available locally around Martlesham Heath and Ipswich.
How long does an MOT take?
A standard MOT takes approximately 45–60 minutes. We carry them out while you wait, so you're welcome to stay in our waiting area or we have a courtesy car available.
My vehicle is undriveable — how do I get it to you?
We can recover your vehicle to our workshop. Just give us a call on 01473 631111 and we'll organise collection as quickly as possible.
What happens if my car fails its MOT?
We offer a free re-test if your vehicle fails. We'll clearly explain what needs to be fixed, give you a written quote, and only proceed with repairs once you've approved the work.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit and debit cards, cash, and bank transfer by BACS.
Is a courtesy car available?
Yes — a courtesy car is available while we work on your vehicle. Please let us know when booking so we can arrange this for you in advance.
Do you work on all makes and models?
Yes, we work on all makes and models of cars and light vehicles. Our technicians are qualified to handle everything from routine servicing to complex diagnostics.
Can I get a free quote for repair work?
Absolutely — we offer free quotations on all repair work. Call us on 01473 631111 or use the online booking form to describe the issue and we'll get back to you promptly.
