Setup Fee — What It Covers & Why We Charge It

Why a setup fee exists

Month one is always the most work — typically 5–20 hours depending on the plan. The setup fee covers that real build time and ensures only serious clients engage.

Clients who pay something upfront are also far more likely to share access, follow through, and stay long term.

  • 2Calendly account, event types, Google Calendar sync and testing

  • 4Make.com review automation build and testing
  • 5Google Analytics and Search Console setup and sitemap submission
  • 6MailerLite account, contacts import, sequences (Growth+)
