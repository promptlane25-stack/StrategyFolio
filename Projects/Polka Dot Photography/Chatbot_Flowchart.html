🔗 BUTTON ACTION LEGEND - WHAT HAPPENS WHEN CUSTOMERS CLICK:
✅ "Yes, let's book" 🔗
What happens: Opens cal.com calendar directly
Customer sees: Calendar widget to select date/time
Result: Instant booking confirmation (no form, no email capture)
❓ "Tell me more" / "What's Included" 📋
What happens: Shows more details or connects to Marta
Customer sees: Additional information based on context
Result: Answer question before booking
📞 "Talk to Marta" / "Talk to Marta Directly"
What happens: Form appears → Customer fills name/phone/email → Message thread opens → Marta responds
Customer sees: Form to fill first, then chat interface for 1-on-1 conversation
Result: Marta has customer contact details and responds to questions/custom requests
⚠️ IMPORTANT FOR CLIENT: Two different paths: "Yes, let's book" opens cal.com directly (no form). "Talk to Marta" shows a form first (name/phone/email) → then message thread opens where Marta can respond directly.
