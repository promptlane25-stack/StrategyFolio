About Leigh

Leigh brings 20+ years of hands-on experience to every job across Suffolk. After 10 years refining his skills at a respected local Ipswich electrical company, he launched Truscott Electrical in January 2021. Based in White House, Ipswich (IP1), he works across all of Suffolk.

The way Leigh works is straightforward: a fixed price agreed before a single wire is touched. No surprise invoices on collection day. Furniture is handled with care, dust is cleared, and Leigh won't leave until the site is tidy. Customers consistently note his punctuality - he turns up when he says he will.

Based in IP1, Leigh covers all of Suffolk with particular focus on Ipswich, Hadleigh, and Woodbridge - and takes on rural and agricultural work throughout the county.
