❓ Common Questions
Frequently Asked Questions

The things customers ask us most — answered honestly.

How long does an MOT take? +
Around 45–60 minutes. We carry out MOTs while you wait — just take a seat. Same-day slots are often available, book online to secure yours.
What if my car fails its MOT? +
We'll explain clearly what failed, provide a written quote, and only start repairs with your approval. Free re-test included once repairs are completed.
Do you work on all makes and models? +
Yes — all makes and models. We have particular expertise with Audi and VW Group vehicles, offering dealer-level knowledge at independent prices.
Can I get a free quote for repairs? +
Always. Free quotations on all repair work. Call 01473 281763 or use the booking form to describe the issue — we'll give you a clear, honest quote before anything starts.
What payment methods do you accept? +
All major credit and debit cards, cash, and bank transfer. Payment plans are also available for larger bills — just ask when you book in.
Do you send updates while working on my car? +
Yes — photos and videos sent directly to you during the repair. You'll always know what's being done and why. No jargon, no surprises.
